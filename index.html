AIM AND VISION

Underserved rural communities in developing India are facing significant barriers to implementing 21st-century skill-set to prepare India’s future workforce for the 4th technology-based industrial revolution. Our research focuses on the self-learning ecosystem to the overall development of an individual’s self-efficacy and mindset in different parts of India by launching an approach for digital literacy covering a variety of topics. Tinkering India Initiative is an open-source replicable model for any school and/or organization to conduct sessions using the content on ‘tinkering.in’ website and spread the movement. The Government of India has announced a New Education Policy in 2020 which has a lot of weightage on digital skills in different skills in early age at school level. We aim to develop students who can become mentors for the next generation of students in their local communities and spread the possibilities for the future of India.
